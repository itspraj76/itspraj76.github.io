Baking a Cake!

  • Time: 65 mins
  • Serves: 10 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour, Sugar, Eggs,
Butter or oil,
Water, Baking soda 		1 cup water,
1/2 vegetable oil,
3 eggs

Step 1: Prepare Baking Pans
Step 2: Allow Ingredients to Reach Room Temperature
Step 3: Preheat the Oven
Step 4: Stir Together Dry Ingredients
Step 5: Combine the Butter and Sugar
Step 6: Add Eggs One at a Time
Step 7: Alternate Adding Dry and Wet Ingredients
Step 8: Pour Batter into Pans and Bake

Directions

  • Heat the oven to 350 F shiny metal or glass pan. Grease all the sides of the pan.

  • Mix wet ingrediants in one bowl. Mix dry ingrediants in another bowl. Add water, oil and eggs in the wet ingrediant bowl.

  • Bake for at least 25-30 minutes, poke the cake with a toothpick, if its not clean bake for a bit longer. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan.

Cakes are alright...but Brownies?!?!

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 10 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter,
Chocolate (finely chopped),
Baking soda, white sugar,
brown sugar, eggs, salt
vanilla extract, all-purpose flour,
baking powder 		10 tbsp butter,
2 ounces unsweetened chocolate,
2 large eggs, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract,
2/3 cup white sugar,
1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup all -purpose flour,
1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Step 1: Line a baking pan with parchment
Step 2: Melt the butter and chocolates in a saucepan
Step 3: Whisk in the sugar
Step 4: Whisk in the eggs
Step 5: Stir in the dry ingredients
Step 6: Spread the batter in the pan
Step 7: Bake the brownies
Step 8: Chill the brownies

Directions

  • Arrange a rack in the middle of the oven and heat to 350°F. Lightly coat the pan and parchment with cooking spray or butter.

  • Add all the wet ingrediants in one bowl is mix them altogether. Add all the dry ingrediants togather and then mix them together as well. And then transfer the batter to the prepared baking pan and spread into an even layer.

  • Bake for at least 25-35 minutes, poke the baked brownie batter with a toothpick, if its not clean, bake for a bit longer. Cool inside refrigerator for at least 1 hour before slicing.

Pizza

  • Time: 8-15 mins
  • Serves: 2-4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Water, yeast,
sugar, all-purpose flour
olive oil, salt,
tomato sauce, cheese 		1 1/2 cups water, 2 1/4 teaspoons dry yeast,
3 3/4 cups bread flour, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon sugar,
2 tablespoons olive oil

Step 1: Gather Ingredients.
Heat the sauce while chopping up the ingredients and preparing the toppings.
Step 2: Prepare the Dough
Step 3: Add the Toppings
Step 4: Heat and then Enjoy!!

Directions

  • Prepare a large floured surface for the dough but start shaping the dough with your hands. Place the dough on the floured surface and, using the palm of your hand, continue to widen the circle until you can't stretch it any further.

  • Let the dough rest for five minutes. For a quick rise, place the dough in a warm place (75°F to 85°F) for 1 1/2 hours. After the pizza dough has risen, you can freeze it to use later.

  • Divide the dough in half. Place each in its own bowl, cover with plastic and let sit for 15 minutes. Prepare your desired toppings. Spread the dough out again with a roller or your hands/fingers. Brush dough with olive oil.

  • Sprinkle pizza peel with corn meal, put flattened dough on top. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings. slide pizza onto pizza stone in oven and bake for about 8-15 minutes.
